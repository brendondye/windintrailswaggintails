About Me

Hello, animal lovers! My name is Brendon and I'm dedicated to providing the best care possible for your furry family members. With prior experience working in veterinary offices, I've gained invaluable knowledge and skills to ensure your pet's well-being. I have a deep-rooted passion for animals, and it's reflected in the love and attention I give to each pet in my care. It's not just a career; it's a calling!

I'm proud to be a pet parent to an English Cream Golden Retriever who can vouch for my expertise! We love taking adventurous hikes, exploring local trails, and cooling off with refreshing swims at the lake. I extend this active lifestyle to the pets in my care, offering activities from jogging to more laid-back playtime in the backyard, all tailored to you and your pet's preferences.

I offer a flexible schedule to fit your needs and enjoy spending time outdoors, which means more fun and exercise for your pup. Feel free to reach out by call, text, or email anytime to discuss how I can make your life easier and your pet's life happier.
